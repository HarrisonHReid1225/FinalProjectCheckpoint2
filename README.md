# Landbound

A 3D survival game built in Unity. Collect resources, manage your hunger, and build a shelter before time runs out.

## Objective
* Collect **5 Ore** and **5 Wood** scattered around the map.
* Keep your **Hunger Bar** filled by eating collected food (Apples, Pears, Berries).
* Press **[B]** to build your house once you have gathered all required materials.
* Win by building the house before the **10-minute timer** runs out or before you starve.

## Controls
* **WASD / Left Stick:** Move Player
* **Mouse:** Look / Camera Control
* **E:** Interact / Collect Resource
* **I:** Toggle Inventory Menu
* **Left Click (in Inventory):** Consume Food
* **B:** Build House (when materials are gathered)

## Features & Systems
* **Interactive Environment:** Dynamic terrain with gatherable resources (Apple, Pear, Berry, Ore, Wood).
* **Inventory System:** Item stacking, grid layout UI, and consumable food mechanics.
* **Survival Challenges:** 10-minute countdown timer, limited inventory capacity, and a decaying hunger system.
* **Game Loop:** Win/Lose screens with restart functionality and objective tracking HUD.
